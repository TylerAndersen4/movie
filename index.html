About the film

Where does the dream stop and the city begin? LA is for Lovers examines modern day Los Angeles. Transportation systems are changing. A city once married to the film industry is making room for other industries. With minimal dialogue, the film relies on striking yet poetic clips of Los Angeles life to tell its story.

Rating: PG-13 / Runtime: 108 minutes / Language: English
